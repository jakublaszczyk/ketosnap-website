Frequently Asked Questions

How does photo analysis work?

KetoSnap uses Google's Gemini AI to analyze your food photos. Simply take a photo of your meal, and our AI will identify the food items and estimate the nutritional content including carbs, protein, fat, and calories. The AI also provides a keto score (1-10) to help you understand how keto-friendly the food is.

How accurate is the macro tracking?

AI-based food analysis provides estimates based on visual recognition. For best results, take clear photos with good lighting and include all items in the frame. For precise tracking, you can manually adjust the values after analysis or use the manual entry feature. Accuracy improves with standard portion sizes and clearly visible food items.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your food logs and profile data are stored locally on your device. Photos sent for analysis are processed in real-time by Google's Gemini AI and are not stored on our servers. We take your privacy seriously - see our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To cancel:
1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
2. Tap your name at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Find KetoSnap and tap it
5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

Can I restore my purchases on a new device?

Yes! If you've purchased a subscription, you can restore it on any device signed in with the same Apple ID. Go to the app's settings or subscription screen and tap "Restore Purchases".

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual food entries by swiping left on them in your food log. To delete all data, you can uninstall the app from your device. Since data is stored locally, uninstalling the app removes all your food logs and profile data.
